## About Benthorne Academy

**Benthorne Academy (Benthorne Scholastic of Finance)** is a specialized financial academy focused on execution-driven trading systems, quantitative decision-making frameworks, and advanced risk control methodologies.

Founded by **Bennett Thorne**, the academy was established with a clear objective:

> Transform trading from subjective speculation into structured execution.

Built around the **Benthorne Execution System**, the academy integrates market structure analysis, behavioral discipline, AI-assisted decision support, and systematic risk management to help individuals navigate increasingly complex financial environments.

---

## Our Mission

At Benthorne Academy, we believe that sustainable trading success is built on execution quality rather than prediction accuracy.

Our mission is to:

* Establish execution as the foundation of trading
* Develop repeatable and measurable decision systems
* Promote disciplined risk management practices
* Reduce emotional interference in financial decision-making
* Enhance trader adaptability through AI-assisted frameworks

## The Benthorne Philosophy

Traditional trading often emphasizes forecasting market direction.

Benthorne Academy takes a different approach.

Our core philosophy is based on a simple principle:

> Execution is only permitted when structural conditions are fully aligned.

This execution-first methodology seeks to improve consistency, discipline, and long-term decision quality across varying market conditions.
